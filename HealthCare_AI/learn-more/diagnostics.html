📋 Common Diagnostic Tests & When To Do Them

🩸 Blood Sugar Test

Do this every 6 months if you are above 40 or have a family history of diabetes.

❤️ Blood Pressure Check

Once every 3 months for adults, more often if you have hypertension.

🧪 Thyroid Test

If you feel tired, gain weight suddenly, or have irregular periods.

🩺 Chest X-Ray

For chronic cough, TB suspicion, or chest pain.

👶 Pregnancy Ultrasound

At least 3 times during pregnancy — in 1st, 2nd, and 3rd trimester.
