⚠️ Safe Medicine Practices

📌 Always Check Expiry

Do not use medicines after expiry. They may lose effect or cause harm.

🥶 Store Properly

Keep medicines away from heat & moisture. Refrigerate only if instructed.

🚫 Avoid Self Medication

Do not take antibiotics or strong drugs without doctor’s advice.

👶 Keep Away from Children

Store medicines in locked cupboards away from kids.
