⛑️ First Aid Tips

💔 Heart Attack

Make the person sit upright, loosen tight clothes, give an aspirin if available, and call emergency immediately.

🩸 Heavy Bleeding

Apply direct pressure with a clean cloth, keep the injured area raised, and do not remove the cloth even if blood soaks.

🔥 Burns

Cool the area with running water for 10–15 minutes, do not apply toothpaste or butter, cover with clean cloth.

🐍 Snake Bite

Keep patient calm, avoid movement, do not cut or suck wound, and rush to hospital immediately.

🌱 Preventive Measures

Emergencies can often be avoided with awareness. Wear helmets, drive carefully, keep first-aid kits at home, avoid open flames near children, and get regular health checkups.
