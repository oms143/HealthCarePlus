Simple Home Remedies

🤧 Cold & Cough

Boil tulsi (holy basil) with ginger; drink warm twice daily.

🤢 Stomach Pain

Drink warm water with ajwain (carom seeds).

🩸 Minor Cuts

Apply turmeric paste to stop bleeding and prevent infection.

🔥 Burns

Apply aloe vera gel gently for quick healing.

🚨 When to See a Doctor Immediately

  • High fever lasting more than 3 days.
  • Severe chest pain or difficulty breathing.
  • Uncontrolled bleeding or deep wounds.
  • Sudden loss of consciousness.
🚑 Call Ambulance (108) 📞 Health Helpline

🌱 Awareness & Preventive Tips

A healthy lifestyle reduces most diseases. Drink clean boiled water, wash hands before eating, and add green vegetables to your diet daily.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I give honey to my 6-month-old baby for cough?
A: No. Honey should only be given to children above 1 year old.

Q: Can turmeric really heal wounds?
A: Yes. It has natural antibacterial properties.

Q: Do I need internet to use chatbot?
A: Not always. Our SMS/IVR-based AI works in low-network areas.
